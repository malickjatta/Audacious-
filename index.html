Descriptions of different type of Samsung smartphone

Samsung Galaxy S10 plus:

The Galaxy S10 Plus has a 6.4-inch screen with borderless Cinematic Display and pin-sharp QHD resolution. This device also has an ultrasonic fingerprint scanner in the screen, wireless Powershare and a spectacular 10 megapixel selfie camera, which also detects depth and can film in 4K quality. At the back is a 12 + 12 + 16 megapixel triple camera.

Samsung Galaxy S10:

The Galaxy S10 has a 6.1-inch screen with borderless Cinematic Display and pin-sharp QHD resolution. This device also has an ultrasonic fingerprint scanner in the screen, wireless Powershare and spectacular 10 megapixel selfie camera and 12 + 12 + 16 megapixel triple camera.!

Samsung Galaxy S10e:

The Galaxy S10e is an inexpensive device with all the cool functionalities of the S10. With a 5.8-inch screen in FHD + resolution, you can see everything clearly. The 10 megapixel selfie camera and 12 + 16 megapixel dual camera, Electrostatic Side Fingerprint and wireless Powershare make the S10e the Next Generation!

Samsung Galaxy S9 Plus:

The screen of the Galaxy S9 Plus is 6.2 inches in size and has a high Quad HD + resolution of 2960x1440 pixels. Now we hear you think, does this huge screen not make for a giant phone? So no! Because the S9 Plus has super thin edges. The Galaxy S9 Plus is therefore no larger than other phablets and smartphones. The screen itself is perhaps the best of the moment. Samsung uses the Super Amoled screen technology, which means higher brightness and more realistic color reproduction. Things like the white balance and viewing angle are also very good. This means that the screen of the new Samsung scores well on all fronts. Topper therefore!
